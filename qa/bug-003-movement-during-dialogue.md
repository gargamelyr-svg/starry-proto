# Bug Report #3
**Title:** Player can move freely during dialogue 

## Steps to reproduce
1. Launch the game
2. Walk-point blank to the NPC
3. Press E to open dialogue
4. Press W, A, S, or D to move while dialogue is open

## Expected behaviour 
Player movement is locked from dialogue initiation until completion.

## Actual behaviour 
Player can move freely while dialogue is open, potentially walking out of the playable area.

## Severity 
Major - player can leave the map or NPC interaction zone mid-conversation
