# Bug Report #1
**Title:** Player jumps when confirming dialogue with Space bar

## Steps to reproduce
1. Launch the game
2. Walk point-blank to the NPC
3. Press E to open dialogue
4. Press Space bar to confirm a dialogue option

## Expected behaviour
Only the dialogue UI updates; no player movement occurs.

## Actual behaviour 
Dialogue confirms and the player jumps simultaneously.

## Severity 
Minor - prototype has a simple flat environment with no harmful consequences from unintended jump.
